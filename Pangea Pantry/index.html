How it works

Your daily dose of culture shipped to your front door !

01

Pick your ingriedients

Waste no time thinking about your next meal! Choose all of your favorite ingredients and let Pangea AI take it from there. We will create a 100% personalized meal; crafted from organic sources, local farmers, and butchers to guarantee you get all the nutrients and vitamins you need shipped to your table, regardless of what diet you follow!

02

Select your weekly meal plan

Once a week, approve a meal plan generated for you by our Pangea AI. Feel free to swap out any ingredients, entire meals, or add your recipe in the mix!

03

Time for delivery

Our chefs in your area will prepare your selected meals everyday and we will deliver them to your front door at a scheduled set time and date that is most convenient for you. You can update your time, location, and date daily to fit your needs.
